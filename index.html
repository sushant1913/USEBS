Why USEB School?

Ujjwal Shishu English Boarding School was Raninagar's first English medium school. A child-centered teaching technique was used in elementary school, which has substantially helped children's learning. Development on all levels: intellectual, emotional, and social. Ujjwal HSEB offers a vibrant, academically stimulating environment. inquiry-based curriculum that is relevant to the needs of our students students. At ujjwal, we place a strong emphasis on cross-disciplinary learning. Our core curriculum is enhanced with learning at every level. Theater, music, and fine arts options, as well as technology and service, are all available. Learning, physical education, and athletics are all important aspects of life.

Our Services

Members of a diverse community of confident, collegial young people, UJJWAL's students make a difference.

Food & Nutrition

The Food Service at ujjwal School aims to provide nutritious lunch and snack options that meet the developmental needs of our students.

Transportation

ujjwal School provides transportation for students in various sections of districts and near india with its buses and vans.

Safety & security

Ujjwal School is committed to the safety and security of our community. We have an extensive crisis response manual that guides our ability to prepare for and respond to a variety of situations.

Hostel

A hostel for both boys and girls is also available, complete with a sanitary canteen and sleeping quarters for hostel students. We also have a warden who supervises and instructs them.
